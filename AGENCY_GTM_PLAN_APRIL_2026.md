# WerkCV Agency Plan - April 2026

## Goal

Close `1 paying agency pilot by April 30, 2026`.

This is not a scale month. This is a validation month.

## What We Know

- The current product converts consumers through `homepage -> templates -> editor -> paid`.
- The current data model is still consumer-first:
  - `User`
  - `CVDocument`
  - `Order`
- There is no real org/workspace/team model yet.
- Dutch recruiter-facing competitors sell a specific workflow, not generic "CV builder" content:
  - `cvForge`: agency page, fixed fee per CV, onboarding, custom template, AVG agreement
  - `CV-Transformer`: CV formatting software for agencies
  - `First2Find`: recruitment software with CV converter angle

Implication:

Do not build an enterprise product before there is paid proof.

## `/agency` Landing Page Brief

### Goal

Turn recruiter and boutique-agency visitors into `pilot conversations`.

### Primary CTA

- `Vraag agency pilot aan`
- Secondary CTA: `Plan eerst een kennismaking`

### Required message

- We solve candidate-CV presentation pain.
- This is a `pilot`, not fake enterprise theater.
- We are strongest for `solo recruiters`, `boutique agencies`, and `outplacement`.
- Pricing starts at `EUR249/month`.
- We use `fair use`, not a risky unlimited promise.

### Sections

1. Hero
   - clear audience
   - clear pain
   - pilot price floor
2. Why this matters
   - manual formatting pain
   - consistent client-facing output
   - faster proposal workflow
   - data/retention clarity
3. What the pilot includes
4. Best-fit / not-fit split
5. How the pilot works
6. FAQ
7. Final CTA

### Claims allowed

- manual onboarding
- branded template
- invoice billing
- fair-use pilot
- priority support
- explicit privacy and retention agreements in pilot setup

### Claims not allowed

- unlimited usage
- enterprise ready
- ATS integration
- full self-serve team workspace
- anonymization as an automated feature unless built

## Outreach Plan

### ICP order

1. solo recruiters
2. boutique agencies (2-10 recruiters)
3. outplacement / reintegration
4. jobcoaches with employer-facing candidate packs

### Target list size

`30-50 accounts` for April.

### Channels

- LinkedIn founder outreach
- direct email
- warm intros where possible

### Weekly pace

- Week 1: build list of 20
- Week 2: send first 15-20 messages
- Week 3: send second batch and follow-ups
- Week 4: close or learn from 3-5 live conversations

### Message angle

Do not pitch "CV builder".

Pitch:

`We help small agencies turn candidate CVs into cleaner branded client-facing documents without subscription traps or a heavy implementation project.`

## Product Changes: Pilot vs Real Workspace

### Can be sold in the pilot now

- agency landing page
- manual onboarding
- invoice-based pilot agreement
- branded template setup
- manual privacy / retention agreement
- manual support process
- tracked CTA clicks and pipeline progress

### Should be built only after paid pilot interest

- `Organization` model
- `Workspace` and `Seat` management
- shared template ownership
- role-based permissions
- invoice lifecycle in product
- usage limits and overage handling
- anonymization flow
- retention controls and deletion tooling
- audit log
