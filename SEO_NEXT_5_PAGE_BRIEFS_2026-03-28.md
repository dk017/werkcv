# Next 5 SEO Page Briefs

Research date: March 28, 2026

Goal: ship 5 BOFU pages that fill current content gaps, fit the existing `cv-gids` architecture, and push users toward the main money page: `/templates`.

Core rule:
- Primary conversion CTA on all 5 pages: `Start je CV in een ATS-proof template` -> `/templates`
- Secondary CTA can be role-specific where useful, but should never replace the primary `/templates` CTA

Research snapshot:
- UWV says the labor market remained tight in 2025, with 58% of employers still affected by shortages and 52% saying they more often hire people who still need training. That strongly supports beginner and no-experience pages.
- CBS reports that in 2024, 76% of Dutch 15-25-year-olds worked, and 55% of them had a temporary contract. That supports student and bijbaan content.
- UWV’s 2025-2026 kansrijke beroepen list includes `magazijn-, expeditiemedewerkers en orderpickers`, `heftruckchauffeurs`, `medewerkers bediening horeca`, `barpersoneel`, `hotelreceptionisten`, and `cateringmedewerkers`.
- UWV says the horeca sector still had more than 22,000 open vacancies in Q4 2025, while remaining highly flexible and student-heavy.
- UWV says administrative professions still had about 72,000 open vacancies in Q2 2025, but also notes that classic administratief/secretaresse work has been declining structurally. That means the parttime admin page should be shipped as a lower-cost derivative page, not treated like a flagship pillar.
