## Rules for batch 1

- Send plain text, not polished marketing copy.
- Keep each email under `140` words before signature.
- Mention the exact page or event you are referencing in the first sentence.
- Offer one asset first, not five.
- Follow up once after `5` to `7` days.
- If someone replies with interest, then send the tailored 2-3 link pack.

## Success threshold for batch 1

Batch 1 is working if you get at least one of these from the first `15` targets:

- `3+` positive replies
- `2+` links or resource inclusions
- `1+` workshop / partner conversation

If batch 1 fails, change the angle before increasing volume.
