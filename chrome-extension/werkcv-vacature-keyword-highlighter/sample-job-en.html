Content Marketing Specialist with SEO and analytics focus

We are hiring a proactive content marketing specialist who is analytical, detail-oriented, and collaborative. This hybrid role supports campaign planning, process improvement, and stakeholder management across the growth team.

What you'll do

  • You will be responsible for content calendars, SEO execution, and social media reporting.
  • You will work closely with recruiting, customer service, and design stakeholders.
  • You will use Google Analytics, WordPress, Excel, and Canva to improve campaign performance.
  • You will support lead generation, content marketing, and project management initiatives.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent working level.
  • Experience with SEO, WordPress, Excel, and social media.
  • Knowledge of Google Analytics, content marketing, and process improvement.
  • Fluent in Dutch and English.
  • You have strong communication skills and a results-driven mindset.

Benefits

  • Full-time or part-time employment type with hybrid working options.
  • Competitive salary, clear compensation bands, and growth opportunities.
  • Visible impact on campaign performance and team output.
