## Rules

- Keep the first message under `120 words`.
- Mention `1` specific thing from their site.
- Pitch `1` pain only.
- Ask for `1` small next step: a `15-minute` call.
- Do not say `unlimited`.
- Do not say `self-serve team platform`.
- Do not say `ATS integration` or `anonymization` unless they ask.

## What To Avoid In Calls

- Do not lead with features.
- Do not promise unlimited volume.
- Do not talk about building a full workspace unless they ask.
- Do not over-explain the consumer CV product.

Lead with:

`Where do candidate CVs still cost you manual time before they are ready to send to a client?`
